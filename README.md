# comical

> 日本語のREADMEはこちらです: [README.ja.md](README.ja.md)

A fun BINGO game designed to help you get to know the members of "Comical Youth Fukui" (@comicalyouth), a youth community based in Fukui, Japan.

## Demo

[Play Comical Member BINGO](https://code4fukui.github.io/comical/)

The game displays a 5x5 BINGO card at the top and a list of community members below. As you "meet" members by clicking buttons, their photos are revealed on the card.

## How to Play

1.  A unique 5x5 BINGO card is generated with photos of the members. The center square is a free space.
2.  Initially, all member squares are hidden and shown in grayscale.
3.  Scroll down to the list of members. When you meet someone, click the "I met them!" (会った！) button under their name.
4.  Their corresponding square(s) on the BINGO card will be revealed in full color.
5.  Your progress is automatically saved in your browser. You can start a new game by using the reset button at the bottom of the page.

## Data Source
